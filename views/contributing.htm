Contributing

Notes on contributing and Pull Requests (PRs):
  1. As usual, I will prioritize PRs which serve the greater good, or are critical.
  2. If I don't accept a PR, it means either:
    • I am currently working on the same thing, or
    • It doesn't serve greater good (perhaps only applies to you or, say, 5% of the users), or
    • It conflicts with existing functionalities

As of now, some of the most wanted features are:
  • AJAX (or promise objects)
  • Lazy loading
